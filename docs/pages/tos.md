# Project Noon: Legal Documentation

## Privacy Policy
**Last Updated:** April 2026

Project Noon is a local-first desktop shell. This policy outlines how we handle your information.

### 1. Data Collection & Use
Project Noon connects to third-party services (like Google) via OAuth to enable shell modules (e.g., music, calendar).
- **OAuth Tokens:** Used solely for local authentication with the provider.
- **Service Data:** Information fetched is used only to populate your UI widgets.

### 2. Local-Only Storage
**All data and tokens are stored exclusively on your local machine.**
- Paths: `~/.config/noon` or `~/.cache/noon`
- No data is uploaded to external servers managed by the Project Noon developers.

### 3. No Third-Party Sharing
We do not sell, trade, or transfer your information. Your data remains strictly between your local machine and the service provider.
